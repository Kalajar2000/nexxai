The gap

Hands-on training

Practical, role-specific workshops where your people build and use AI on their own real tasks, not generic theory or slideware.

Change management

The program

How an upskilling engagement runs

1

Assess

We map your team's current AI fluency, tools, and workflows to find the highest-leverage skills to build first.

2

Train

Role-specific, hands-on sessions where people apply AI to their own day-to-day work, from ops to marketing to engineering.

3

Apply

Guided pilots on live tasks, so new skills translate into real output and quick, visible wins.

4

Embed

We install playbooks, internal champions, and prompt templates so the new way of working becomes the default.

5

Measure

We track adoption and time saved, then iterate the program around what is driving the most value.
