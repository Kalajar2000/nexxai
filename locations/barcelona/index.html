Barcelona FAQ

Working with our Spain hub

Do you work with companies based in Spain and the EU?

Yes. Barcelona is one of our core delivery hubs. We work with startups and enterprises across Spain and the wider European Union, and we operate in Central European Time for a full working-day overlap.

Is your work GDPR-compliant?

Yes. Building from the EU, GDPR and data-residency are part of how we design systems - data handling, consent and storage are addressed from the first architecture decision, not bolted on later.

What languages does the Barcelona team work in?

We work in Spanish and English, so we can match how your team and your customers actually communicate.

What do you build from Barcelona?

Production AI (agents, ML models and RAG assistants), custom software and SaaS, and native and cross-platform mobile and web apps - from first idea to shipped product.
