Do I need technical knowledge to work with NEXXAI?

Not at all. We handle all the technical aspects and translate everything into plain language. Your team will be able to use the solutions without any technical background.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see their first results within 4–8 weeks of starting. We focus on quick wins that deliver immediate value while building toward bigger solutions.
